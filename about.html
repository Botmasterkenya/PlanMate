About PlanMate

PlanMate was built out of the frustration of managing study groups, clubs, and fellowships in endless, chaotic group chats. We believe that organizing events should be as beautiful and seamless as the moments you share together.

Our Mission

To provide a single, premium platform where organizing, tracking, and executing group activities becomes a delightful experience rather than a tedious chore.

Why Us?

Unlike heavy enterprise tools or scattered messaging apps, PlanMate specifically targets the casual yet organized needs of clubs, fellowships, and student groups.
